alias:: Longs

- ---
- 64-bit signed [[Integer]]
- Range (inclusive)
	- Minimum
		- `-9,223,372,036,854,775,808`
	- Maximum
		- `9,223,372,036,854,775,807`
- ---
- Create a new [[Long]]
	- Literaly notation
		- Any integral number in the range above
		- ``` clojure
		  0
		  1
		  42
		  1234567890
		  ```
	- [[(long)]]
- ---
- [[Predicates]]
	- [[(long?)]]
	- [[(number?)]]
- ---
- Also see [[Integer]]
